Why is Princess Bride one of the Best Films of all Time?

Welcome to my Website!

There are many reasons to consider Princess Bride as one of the best films ever made. The plot has many elements within it: action, romance, revenge and more can be found in the film. The characters are extremely enjoyable to watch and entrance you into the film. This film is an excellent action movie, especially the swordfighting scene on the Cliffs of Insanity; this scene is one of my favorite fight scenes ever. The Cliffs of Insanity actually exists as the Cliffs of Moher in Ireland; the picture is of these cliffs. This film has been selected by the Library of Congress for preservation as being "culturally, historically or aesthetically significant"¹ along with others like Who Framed Roger Rabbit and Star Wars: A New Hope. These are some of my reasons for enjoying the film; why not watch it today on Disney+, Youtube, or Amazon Prime?
